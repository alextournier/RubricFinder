# Repertorium Publicum

## Overview

- **Author**: Vladimir Polony (Slovakia)
- **First release**: 2008
- **License**: GPL v3
- **Language**: English
- **Version in OOREP dump**: 0.5
- **Current version**: 0.9 (238,000 remedy additions)

## Description

Repertorium Publicum is the first free, modern open-source homeopathic repertory. It is based on Kent's Repertory structure, enhanced with symptom groups from Boenninghausen's repertory.

Features:
- Detailed symptoms describing the full symptom in its totality
- "Supergroups" of symptoms that meaningfully group locations, aggravations, ameliorations or phenomena
- 442 extra remedies beyond Kent's original
- Over 1,000 symptoms of Carcinosinum

## About the Author

Vladimir Polony was born in Slovakia in 1978. He holds a master's degree in economics from Matej Bell University and is also a computer programmer.

After working for a commercial homeopathic software company, he decided to create the first open-source alternative. His goal: "to make homeopathy accessible to everyone."

### Other works by Polony:
